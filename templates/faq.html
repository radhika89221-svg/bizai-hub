Why do I need to log in?

Login keeps your tool history, quota usage, dashboard activity, and protected APIs tied to your account.

What happens when I hit my daily quota?

The app blocks new AI actions for the rest of the day and resets automatically on the next quota day.

How does image generation work now?

BizGenius AI creates an image job, polls progress, tries multiple providers in fallback order, and reuses cached images when the same prompt and size already exist.

Can I upload files in audio tools?

Yes. You can now upload transcript files like .txt, .md, and .csv for backend processing. Direct binary audio uploads still need a dedicated server-side transcription provider, so the app gives you a clear fallback message when one is not configured.

Where can I find my saved work?

Each tool has its own recent history, and your dashboard shows recent cross-tool activity with export options.
