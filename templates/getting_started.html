2. Start with one of the core tools

  • Use Content Writer for marketing emails, proposals, and product copy.
  • Use Image Generator for logos, banners, posters, and branded visuals.
  • Use Chatbot for strategy questions and quick business advice.
  • Use Audio Tools for podcast scripts, notes, and transcript processing.
Content Writer Image Generator Audio Tools

3. Save, revisit, and export your work

Your recent generations are stored in history panels and in your dashboard. You can also export saved work from the dashboard in plain text or JSON format.

4. Use the fallbacks built into the app

If image generation providers are busy, the app now retries across providers and reuses cached images when the same prompt was already generated. Audio tools also support transcript-file uploads when direct browser speech tools are not enough.
